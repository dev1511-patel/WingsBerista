1. Create a realistic cafe-style food image with cinematic lighting and high contrast.

Canvas:

* Aspect ratio: 4:5 (1080x1350, Instagram post)
* Vertical composition

🚨 IMPORTANT STYLE DIRECTION:

* Keep the scene NATURAL like a real cafe
* BUT apply CINEMATIC DARK COLOR GRADING (not flat or dull)
* Increase contrast: deep shadows + bright highlights
* Colors should be rich, especially RED and golden tones

Food:

* Place the food plate at center
* Keep the original food, plate, and presentation EXACTLY the same (no changes)
* Add slight gloss and highlight to make food look crispy and fresh
* Add realistic steam rising from the food (soft but visible)

Table:

* Red and black checkered cloth
* Keep it realistic but slightly darker in tone

Background:

* Realistic cafe interior (slightly blurred / bokeh)
* Maintain RED wall theme
* Add warm hanging lights (soft glow)
* Background should be darker than food (depth effect)

Logo:

* Place Wing Berista chicken logo on back wall
* Keep logo SMALL and balanced
* Add soft glow (not too strong, natural signboard feel)

Lighting:

* Use directional lighting (from left or right side)
* Food should be the brightest subject
* Background slightly dim and soft

Color Grading (VERY IMPORTANT):

* Cinematic tone
* Deep blacks, rich reds, warm highlights
* Slight vignette (dark corners)
* High dynamic range (HDR look)

Quality:

* Ultra realistic, DSLR quality
* Professional food photography
* Sharp focus on food, blurred background

🚫 DO NOT:

* Do NOT make lighting flat or overexposed
* Do NOT make colors washed out
* Do NOT oversize the logo
* Do NOT change food structure

Final Look:

* Should feel like a real cafe photo but with premium cinematic contrast and depth
* Dark, rich, and visually appealing (Instagram-ready)
